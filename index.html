Andika Typeface

A Sans-Serif Typeface

"Andika is a sans serif, unicode-compliant font designed especially for literacy use, taking into account the needs of beginning readers. The font is on clear, easy-to-perceive letterforms that will not be readily confused with one another. As a sans-serif font, it is often perferred to teach people to read for its simpler and less cluttered forms."
